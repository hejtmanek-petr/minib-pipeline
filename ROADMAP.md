# Roadmap / Future Ideas

1. **Email notifications** — alert HQ when a dealer adds a comment or changes a project status; weekly pipeline digest email.
2. **Map view** — plot projects on a world map (Leaflet.js), bubble size proportional to EUR value.
3. **Automatic comment translation** — one-click translation of Turkish (or other) comments into EN/CS for HQ.
4. **PWA / mobile app** — manifest + service worker, add-to-home-screen, offline dashboard cache.
5. **Forecast module** — projected revenue (win probability × MINIB price), broken down by month.
6. **AI competitor intelligence** — when a competitor (Kapmann, Varmann, Isoterm, Stekon, ...) is mentioned in a comment, AI generates a short competitive brief automatically.
7. **Approval workflow** — dealer proposes a discount/special price, HQ approves directly in the app.
8. **Attachments** — attach PDFs (technical docs, price offers) to a project.
9. **Deadline alerts** — highlight projects whose `estimated_decision_date` is in the past while still `active` (partially implemented in the Order Timeline report).
10. **Multi-region expansion** — support additional Excel sheets (MEA, Europe, Americas) via a region/sheet dropdown.
11. **ERP/CRM integration** — two-way sync with HubSpot or MINIB's internal systems.
12. **Dark mode** — for users working evenings across time zones.
13. **Bulk editing** — select multiple projects in the dashboard table and apply a status/phase change to all of them at once.
